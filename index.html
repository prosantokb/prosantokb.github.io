about me
Prosanto K. Barman

Builder at the intersection of
hardware & software

I'm a 3rd-year CSE student at Dhaka University of Engineering & Technology (DUET), Gazipur. I chose CSE because it gives me tools to build products that reach the world — and eventually launch a business around them.

My work lives where firmware meets with hardware. I build complete systems: sensors, microcontrollers, cloud sync, and Flutter interfaces — the whole stack. As Software Division Lead of DUET Robotics Club, I lead a team of developers and represent DUET in national competitions.

My long-term goal is to engineer intelligent physical products — combining robotics, AI, and embedded hardware — and build a startup around them.
